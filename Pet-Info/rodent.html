RODENT

Information about Rodent

  • Scientific classification

    Kingdom: Animalia
    Phylum: Chordata
    Class: Mammalia
    (unranked): Glires
    Order: Rodentia

  • Type of rodent


    Hamster

    Hamsters are rodents. Its small shape makes it easy for the hamster to carry around and does not require a cage that is too large to care for. Hamsters have thick, long hair, and their hair has various colors depending on the hamster species.

    Food for Hamster,
    Hamsters are omnivorous creatures. Their food is usually rice grains, but also includes fresh fruit, roots, green parts of plants, invertebrates and some other small animals (insects such as grasshoppers).
    However, not all foods are suitable for hamsters. Some foods, such as the poisonous leaves of tomatoes, garlic, citrus are the most dangerous foods for the health of hamsters. Too much hamster giving vegetables causes their tails to wet and causes them to die within 24 hours.
    If we want to keep hamsters, the safest thing is to provide food in the form of fresh grains. Choose grains that are not preserved but are free of lice and coloring agents. Do not give foods that contain sugar or salt. Some types of fresh leaves may not be harmful to hamsters, but most leaves are contaminated with pesticides. Although a little, hamsters are susceptible to pesticides, so the vegetables given to hamsters must be of high quality. Instant food is also not good given to hamsters because they usually contain preservatives. Salt containing foods can cause hair loss in the hamster's hair.

    Mini hedgehog

    Hedgehog are rodents (Rodentia) which have thick hair and sharp spines. Their weapons and shields are all over the outer surface of their skin in the form of hollow thorns that can harden in an instant.
    These mammals are timid and therefore protect themselves quickly if they feel threatened. To protect themselves, they will make their bodies into balls so that all the feathers will be outside. Just like other mammals, this animal is a mammal and milk is his first food until he is old enough.

    Food for mini hegdog,
    Most mini hedgehogs are given food that has been sold on the market such as cat and dog food in the form of biscuits, this is not a problem as long as we choose carefully so that hedgies get food that has good quality protein, low fat, and provides a source of fiber sufficient. Porcupines themselves need about 70-100 calories per day, Hedgies are given a diet with cat food, especially those containing fiber in it (rice, beans, baby food, pasta, as well as mealworms and crickets). Mixing in baby oatmeal flakes is the best way to add fiber

    Rabbit

    Rabbits are small mammals. Rabbits are herbivores that forage by eating leaves and plants in grasses, shrubs and weeds. Therefore, rabbit foods contain large amounts of cellulose, which is difficult to digest. Rabbits break down this cellulose by gastric fermentation, with the help of decomposing bacteria.
